## Executive Summary

BlindSpot occupies a **genuinely novel position** in the AI career tools market. No competitor combines skill decay analysis, competence illusion detection, career risk scoring, and dual-path career projection. The "career intelligence" category barely exists for individuals — enterprise platforms (Eightfold AI at $2.1B+, Gloat at $192M, BetterUp at $628M) have this capability but charge enterprise prices ($3,000-5,000/employee/year). BlindSpot can democratize this.

## 5. Risks and Caveats

1. **LinkedIn could build this**: They have the data and distribution. Mitigation: move fast, build proprietary models.
2. **Data quality is critical**: Static CSV/JSON won't scale. Must integrate real APIs.
3. **B2C vs B2B pivot question**: Most HR tech VCs prefer B2B. Be prepared to articulate enterprise angle.
4. **Market sizing variance**: 20-40% variance between research firms is normal. Numbers are directional.
5. **Regulatory landscape evolving**: 400+ state AI bills introduced in 2024. BlindSpot is consumer-facing (lower risk than employer-facing tools), but monitor.
